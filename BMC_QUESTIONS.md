# Full Question Set (BMC + Scaling)

**Customer Segments**
1. Who is your primary customer persona? Demographics, location (India/global?), pains, desires?
2. Secondary segments? Market size estimate?

**Value Propositions**
3. What exact jobs/pains/gains does your solution solve?
4. Why is it 10x better than alternatives?

**Channels**
5. How will customers discover, evaluate, buy, and receive your product?

**Customer Relationships**
6. What type of relationship (self-service, personal assistance, automated, community)?

**Revenue Streams**
7. Pricing models? Expected pricing? Recurring vs one-time?

**Key Resources**
8. What physical/intellectual/human/financial resources do you need?

**Key Activities**
9. What must happen daily/weekly to deliver value?

**Key Partners**
10. Who are your suppliers, allies, co-creators?

**Cost Structure**
11. Major fixed and variable costs?

**Scaling Deep-Dive**
12. Current stage (idea/MVP/launch)? Team size & skills?
13. Budget/funding status and runway?
14. 6-month, 1-year, 3-year goals (revenue/users)?
15. Biggest scaling barriers you foresee?
16. Tech/ops plan for 10x growth?
17. Customer acquisition plan & CAC target?
18. Retention & LTV strategy?
19. Competitive moat?
20. Any India-specific factors (regulations, payments, logistics)?
